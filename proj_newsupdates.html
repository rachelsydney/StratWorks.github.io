Stratworks News Updates

  • Stratworks Celebrates 25 Years of Success

    Stratworks, the multi-award winning strategic marketing communications agency, is celebrating its 25th anniversary. The agency, which is fully independent and wholly Filipino-owned, has had a remarkable journey over the past 25 years.

  • Stratworks Continues to Inspire with Award-Winning Campaigns

    Despite the challenges of the past year, Stratworks has continued to deliver impactful and award-winning campaigns. The agency's commitment to storytelling that inspires courage and ignites brand love has been recognized with over 222 awards and counting.

  • Stratworks Expands Reach with Global Partnerships

    Stratworks has expanded its reach beyond the Philippines, forming partnerships with brands and organizations around the world. These partnerships have allowed the agency to tell stories that resonate with audiences on a global scale.

  • Stratworks Champions Creativity and Innovation

    Stratworks is known for championing creativity and innovation in the marketing communications industry. The agency's commitment to pushing boundaries and exploring new ideas has led to groundbreaking campaigns that have captured the imagination of audiences worldwide.
